## 2. Product context

**Oasis** is an iOS sleep/focus app built on 20 hand-recorded nature sounds with 3D audio, binaural modes and a sleep timer. iOS 18+ only, SwiftUI, Liquid Glass UI.

**Category:** Sleep Sounds / White Noise / Focus Audio. Dominated by subscription apps (Calm, Headspace, Portal, Rainy Mood).

**The one moat that matters for conversion in this category:**

> **No subscription. Ever. One-time purchase — yours forever.**

This is the single most powerful claim in a subscription-saturated category. Every screenshot either reinforces it or earns the right not to.

**Secondary moats (use sparingly):**
- 20 sounds **hand-recorded on location** (authenticity)
- **3D audio** placement — sounds around you, not just in front (differentiator)
- **100% offline** — no account, no tracking, no ads
- **Every future Premium sound included** — you pay once, you get everything

**Do not write:**
- Any exclamation marks (tone is calm, not hyped)
- "Best / #1 / revolutionary" (Apple rejects superlatives)
- "Free trial" or "Free to try" (we are freemium, not trial)
- Anything implying subscription

### ⚠ Important: animations are frozen in these captures

Under UI-test automation, `WaveformSignatureLine` and `AnimatedLiquidAura` are intentionally paused (otherwise XCUITest can't reach quiescence and the run takes 3+ hours). That means:

- The **header waveform** shows one deterministic wave shape, not a motion blur or a flat line.
- The **animated liquid backdrop** shows one static frame instead of shifting colour.

This is by design — visually it reads as "a nice still instant" of an otherwise live UI. **Do not try to add motion blur, multi-frame composites, or "live waveform" overlays in Figma** to simulate movement; the single captured frame is the ground truth.

### 5.4 Headline length constraint

Every headline must fit **≤ 2 lines at 88pt within 1080pt width**. That's roughly **≤ 30 characters per line**, **≤ 60 characters total**. Longer languages (DE, ES sometimes) may need either a step down to 78pt OR a slightly truncated translation. Prefer truncation — keep the scale consistent.

### 5.5 What *not* to decorate with

- No emoji in headlines
- No flag icons (they render as tofu boxes in some simulator locales)
- No "As seen in …" badges
- No price tag / badge
- No star ratings (we don't fake social proof)

## 10. Judgement calls

Where this brief doesn't prescribe, favour:

- **Minimal over maximal** — if in doubt, remove the element
- **Typography over illustration** — no icons, no dingbats

- **One idea per slide** — never bullet-list features inside a screenshot

When tempted to add "As featured in…" or "★★★★★" or "4.8/5" — don't. We don't fake social proof.
