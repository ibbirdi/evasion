Review notes for Oasis macOS 1.0.0

This submission is the macOS adaptation of the existing Oasis iOS app. It uses the same bundle ID, sound catalogue, audio engine, localization, RevenueCat entitlement, and one-time lifetime Premium purchase model as the iOS version.

Oasis for macOS is intentionally designed as a native menu bar app. After launch, please click the Oasis icon in the macOS menu bar to open the mixer panel. There is no separate main app window.

The macOS version keeps the same core experience as iOS:
- 35 bundled real-world nature sounds for sleep, focus, relaxation, and ambience.
- Per-sound volume mixing and sound placement.
- Binaural modes, timers, shuffle, saved mixes, and sound details.
- Offline-first use after installation.
- No account or login required.

To review the in-app purchase:
- Open Oasis from the macOS menu bar icon.
- Click any locked Premium feature, such as a premium sound, advanced binaural mode, longer timer, or additional saved mix.
- The custom Premium panel will appear.
- The purchase is a one-time lifetime unlock shared with the existing Oasis iOS app entitlement.
- Restore Purchases is available from the Premium panel.
